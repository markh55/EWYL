About Us

Earn While You Learn is Wales’ leading training provider, we offer a wide range of work-based learning, employability programmes, and industry-focused training. Highlights include the Jobs Growth Wales+ initiative and funded courses to enhance skills and careers. Our tailored commercial training helps individuals and businesses stay competitive. Whether advancing your career or workforce, we provide comprehensive support for growth.


Is an apprenticship for you?

You get to kick-start or boost your career with an apprenticeship! It’s a hands-on program where you build real-world skills, earn recognized qualifications (levels 2-5), and get paid while learning. As an apprentice, you’ll enjoy:

  • A salary
  • Paid holidays
  • Training and qualifications
  • Job-specific skills
  • Employer-valued expertise
  • Excellent progression opportunities
